Hey Nasrs! Wanna play Battleship?

If so, read on.

About

This will be one-player Battleship: you are the guessers and we control the ships. So your objective is to sink all of our ships in the fewest moves possible.

We've hidden three ships on our board: one Battleship (occupies 4 spaces), one Submarine (3), and one Destroyer (2). The board is made up of the panes within our lower deck slider: 5 rows (labeled A-E) by 6 columns (labeled 1-6).

Playing

  1. Send us a guess (letter + number) in any way you choose (text, posted in a window, etc.)
  2. We'll show you the result on our board.
