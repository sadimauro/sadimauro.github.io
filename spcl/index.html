Severna Park Champions League

About

This is a Champions League-style competition for four players, starting at the quarterfinal stage, played with Topps Match Attax. May the strongest team win, and earn the glory of millions far and wide.

Playing

  1. Send us a guess (letter + number) in any way you choose (text, posted in a window, etc.)
  2. We'll show you the result on our board.
