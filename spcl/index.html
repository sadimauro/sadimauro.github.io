Severna Park Champions League

About

This is a Champions League-style competition for four players, played with Topps Match Attax trading cards. The top two teams emerging from the group stage will play a two-match finale, with the team with top aggregate the victor.

May the strongest team win and earn the glory and admiration of millions far and wide.

Playing

Setup

  • Each player will pick a team of 14 players (includes three subs) with salaries totaling 100M euros or less. An impartial, independent party should verify the integrity of the bookkeeping.
  • The 14-player team will be used throughout all of the matches during the tournament, though the selected three subs can change per match.
  • Each player should submit a team name, and (optionally) a unique team logo, to the league administrator.

Per Match

  • From his 14-player team, each player will prepare 11 starters and 3 substitutes.
  • Play continues according to Topps Match Attax rules. The home team gets first choice (card, and attack or defend).
  • Goals for/against will be tallied.
